About Islam

Islam is a monotheistic Abrahamic religion based on the teachings of Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him). The Quran is the final revelation of Allah (God) guiding Muslims in every aspect of life.

Key principles of Islam include faith in one God, prayer, fasting, charity, and pilgrimage. These form the Five Pillars of Islam, essential practices for every Muslim.

Islam emphasizes peace, justice, compassion, and leading a righteous life according to the Quran and Hadith (sayings and actions of the Prophet).
